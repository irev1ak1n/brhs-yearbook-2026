Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about the BRHS Yearbook.

How do I buy a yearbook?

You can buy a yearbook through the Yearbook website’s ordering page.

When is the deadline to order?

Ordering deadlines will be posted on the website and shared through school updates.

Can students submit their own photos?

Yes. Students may be able to submit photos for consideration

When will yearbooks be distributed?

Yearbooks are usually distributed near the end of the school year

Who should I contact if I have a question?

For any questions about orders, submissions, or yearbook information, please use the Contact page and send a message to the yearbook team.
